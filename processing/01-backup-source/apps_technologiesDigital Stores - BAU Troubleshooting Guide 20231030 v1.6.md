## Page 3

TORY
- Mis-localised run
Clean TORY lasers/check wheels for debris > Check TORY run path map to identify where problem occurred > Complete pre-run checklist prior to the next run.

- TORY is hitting shroud at the start of run
Clean TORY lasers/check wheels for debris > If consecutive runs failed due to hitting shroud at start of run raise with "Store Support".

- TORY keeps failing to dock after running
Check charging area for debris, clean if required > Return to charger > If problem repeats raise with "Store Support"

- Lost TORY keys
Replacement TORY keys can be ordered via RTB (Spare Parts).

HAND SCANNER
- Unable to connect to hand scanner to PDT
1: Turn on hand scanner (Hold power button, top right, green light wait for a beep) > Hold on Bluetooth button (bottom left, flashing blue light until flashing).

2: Log in to the RFID APP> Click on hand-scanner symbol> Select Zebra > Start Scan > Continuously hold the yellow trigger > click the correct device ID (located under the wand) >Click yes connect > Continue holding yellow trigger, follow the prompts > Device will give a beep once successfully connected.

- Completing a handscan to capture stock that Tory is unable to
Pair handscan as above. Refer to training guide.

POWER PACK (Large printer)
- Seeing a triangle with an "!" on the screen
Unplug the printer from the AC > Turn AC power on > Plug printer back into AC.

PRINTER (Portable)
- Printer not connecting to network
Reboot Printer in New Area of Stockroom or Shopfloor > Contact store support.

- RFID label ordering
Order via Bluestar

Contact "Store Support" 1800 033 534

## Page 4

PRINTER (Large)
Issue: Printing out misaligned tags
Solution: Perform a manual calibration > Click menu on the printer > click the printer icon > click "Manual Calibration" > Click start calibration > There will be a 16-step visual guide > Follow the prompts.
Link: https://intranet.core.kmtltd.net.au/public/download.jsp?id=448654

PRINTER (Large)
Issue: I cannot find the IP address
Solution: Click on "Printer Info" in top right corner, IP address is under "Active IP (WLAN)".
Link: https://intranet.core.kmtltd.net.au/public/download.jsp?id=448654

PRINTER (Large)
Issue: The tags are sliding out to the right
Solution: Ensure the black lever (it has a yellow bottom) is pushed in as far as it can go so it is holding the tags in place.
Link: https://intranet.core.kmtltd.net.au/public/download.jsp?id=448654

PRINTER (Large)
Issue: The printer is printing out VOID stickers
Solution: Perform an RFID calibration. Click menu on the printer > Click the 2nd icon from the left (RFID image) > Ensure tags are fed out of the printer > Click RFID calibrate (this will take 5-10 minutes) wait until done > Once done click the tick.
Link: https://intranet.core.kmtltd.net.au/public/download.jsp?id=448654

PRINTER (Large)
Issue: The small calibration has stopped happening when I turn the printer on
Solution: On the printer go to settings > Change "Power Up Action" to calibrate > Restart the printer to confirm it is working.
Link: https://intranet.core.kmtltd.net.au/public/download.jsp?id=448654

PRINTER (Large)
Issue: The small calibration has stopped happening when I close the printer lid
Solution: On the printer, go to settings > Change "Head Close Action" to calibrate.
Link: https://intranet.core.kmtltd.net.au/public/download.jsp?id=448654

PRINTER (Large)
Issue: Printer is printing out faint labels
Solution: On the Printer, go to settings > Print Quality > Darkness set to 25-30.

PRINTER (Both)
Issue: Thermal ribbon or RFID label ordering
Solution: Order via Bluestar: Kmart Handheld Printer RFID Sticker Product ID: KMA95026392 (Portable) Kmart Thermal Ribbon Roll Product ID: KMA95026390 (Large) Kmart Blank RFID Labels Product ID: KMA95026389 (Large)

PRINTER (Both)
Issue: RFID app not connecting to printer
Solution: Restart Printer & PDT > Contact "Store Support"

RFID APP
- Backfill data will not load (TORY was interrupted)
Check Tory run quality was over 90% > If under 90%, no backfill will generate > If over 90% > Reboot PDT > Try alternative PDT. Next Step: Menu, tasks, play button on synchronize and remaining play buttons > refresh app > Check backfill > If still no backfill contact ITSD.

RFID APP
- Backfill data will not load (No run issues identified)
Check above 2 steps first > Click inventory summary tile > Ensure inventory % is above 40% (if under 40% no backfill will be generated > Click save and finalize > Wait 15 minutes > If still no backfill, raise with "Store Support".

RFID APP
- Backfill data will not load (Inventory summary page tile shows errors)
Check all above steps first > If error symbol is orange, data is still uploading (wait up to 1 hour and try again) > Raise with ITSD > If error symbol is red, data has failed to upload > Raise with "Store Support".

RFID APP
- RFID app not on PDT
Raise with "Store Support"

RFID APP
- PDT freezes when loading data
Reboot PDT > Try another PDT > Raise with "Store support."

Store Operations

Depending on run quality and inventory levels store may still get backfill data for selling floor level. Check to see if backfill has loaded. Set reminders for management team to move robot on time. Contact store support if a new/later schedule is required for 2nd level.

Notify ZM and store support. Engage EM stop buttons.

Store Operations

Locate and fill stock where possible. Coach team to ensure stock is left in the right containers. Contact "Store Support" to make any required container adjustments.
