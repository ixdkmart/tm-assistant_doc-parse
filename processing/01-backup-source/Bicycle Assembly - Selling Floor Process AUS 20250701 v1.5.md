Bicycle Assembly Process
Selling Area

Store Area: Leisure Manager

When work is performed:
• Prior to visit from Hunter Service representative
• During and post visit from Hunter Service representative
• When selling an assembled bicycle to customer
• Maintaining standards of the bicycle unit

Activity Steps:

Prior to visit:
• Perform a visual check of bicycle unit to determine number of bicycle checklists requiring replacement.
• Ensure appropriate space available for the assembly of bicycles (not on the selling area).
• Bicycles requiring assembly and/or repair are ready and accessible for the Hunter Service representative.
• Ensure designated work area is safe and free from obstructions.

During visit:
• Clearly communicate work required to Hunter Service representative – whether it is repair work or assembly of bicycles and number of assembly checklists required to be replaced as per visual check.
• Sign work invoices promptly for completion of agreed works ensuring:
    - All bicycles on display unit have a completed Hunter Service Assembly Checklist attached by cable ties to the top tube (handle bar) of the bicycle.
    - The manufacturer warranty and instruction booklet is attached to the bicycle seat post of assembled bicycles.
• Discuss and come to an agreement on the following weeks assembly and/or repair requirements including any promotional and or lay-by potential requirements.
• Discuss the need for additional visits in the same week if required due to a higher than normal sales expectations.

Ongoing Post Visit (maintaining bicycle unit standard):
• Rectify missing Point of Sale material from bicycle display unit as discussed with the Hunter Service representative.
• Point of Sale installed as required highlighting to the customer:
    - The assembly charge of $16.50.
    - Bicycles are sold in cartons and display models are not for sale. (Bicycles are to remain in the unit).
• Hunter Service Assembly Checklist completed by an authorised Hunter Service representative is attached to the top tube (handle bar) of the bicycle of all display bicycles.
• Manufacturer warranty and instruction booklet is attached to the bicycle seat post of all assembled bicycles.

When selling a display or sample bicycle:
• The customer is not charged the bicycle assembly fee when:
    - The display model is a clearance item.
    - When the display model is the last stock on hand of the model.
• Ensure a K412 Soiled & Damaged Stock Sticker (keycode 36411863) is displayed on any previously returned bicycle that is being sold including a completed Hunter Service Assembly Checklist, manufacture warranty and instruction booklet.

Charging Requirements:
• The customer is charged the $16.50 assembly fee when:
    - Specifically requesting to purchase a display model.
    - Requested to have a model assembled by the Hunter Service representative.
• The customer is not charged for any bicycle repair covered by the warranty conditions.

Contacts
Contact Hunter Service (Service Manager) through the 1800 632 792 number for any urgent assembly and/or repair needs or issues that require resolution ensuring:

When A Call Is Answered By Hunter Service (Service Manager):
• The person calling is to introduce him or herself by name, provide store number and store name.
• State clearly what the issue and/or question relate to in a professional and polite manner.
• Provide any further information that is requested by the Service Manager that will assist in resolving the issue and/or query.

When A Call Requires A Message To Be Left:
• The person calling is to introduce himself or herself by name, provide store number store name.
• State clearly what the issue and/or question relate to in a professional and polite manner.

Activity Measurement
• Each assembled bicycle will have a 'Hunter Service Assembly Checklist' attached.
• Assembly and repair of all brands of bicycles carried out by the authorised Hunter Service representative.
• Bicycle display unit maintained to the required Standard of Presentation.
• All bicycles sold will conform to the Hunter Service Assembly Checklist criteria.
