## Page 1

FLEXIPOS - AirPay Tap

The TC52 PDT's (these PDT's commence with a 200..PDT number) have been updated with Airpay Tap, this will enable all FlexiPOS transactions to be processed by tapping the EFT card directly on the back of the PDT when selecting 'Pay @ Card Reader' to complete the transaction.

Note:
The AP3 pinpads or TC51's will no longer be used for FlexiPOS transactions.

When using FlexiPOS for the first time after the upgrade to Airpay Tap, an initial one of process will need to be performed to Verify permissions required to perform the Airpay tap transactions.

There is no transaction limit on FlexiPOS Airpay Tap transactions.

The 'Tap and Go limit' (depending on customers bank) is $100.00, for transactions over this amount a new 'Enter Pin' screen will be displayed allowing the customer to enter their pin to complete the transaction.

There is no AP3 (pinpad) required when performing an AirPay tap transaction.

AirPay Tap can only be used on a TC52 PDT, these PDT's commence with 20 eg. KMPD201323

Transaction Limitations
• Cash Tender
• Mixed Tenders
• E Vouchers
• Suspend/Retrieve a transaction – A new QR Code can be produced where a transaction cannot be completed on FlexiPOS.
• Gift Cards/egift cards (purchase and redemption)
• Flybuys Dollars
• iTunes/3rd Party Gift Cards
• Price Required and Visual Verify items
• Layby
• Refunds
• Recharge items
• Donations
• Price Overrides over $10.00 (no additional receipt will be produced)
• Pickup and Delivery receipts
• Cards that require insert and swipe
• Diners/Union Pay
• OnePass barcodes are not accepted on FlexiPOS

Actions Required prior to using FlexiPOS AirPay Tap
• Check the FlexiPOS version number on the Login screen is V2.1.7
• Check the AirPay Tap version number on settings menu is 1.4.17 (37)
