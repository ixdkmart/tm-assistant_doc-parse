Bicycle Assembly Process Registers

Store Area
Front End and Internal Registers

When work is performed
• Customer presents assembled bicycle at front end or internal register for purchase.

Activity Steps
Selling a bicycle:
• Customer Service Operator to remove Hunter Service Assembly Checklist from all assembled bicycles sold.
• Customer Service Operator to complete the following information on the store copy of the Hunter Service Assembly Checklist using a medium black pen:
    - Purchaser's name
    - Purchaser's address
    - Phone number
    - Purchase date
    - Store number
• The store portion of the checklist to be retained and placed in the register drawer and forwarded to the Cash Office for filing.
• The $16.50 assembly fee is processed through the register as non-merchandise code 2-repair sale. Unless advised otherwise, all customers presenting an assembled bicycle for purchase is to be charged the assembly fee.
• Customers are not charged for any bicycle repair covered by the warranty conditions.
• An assemble bicycle is not to be sold that does not have an assembly checklist completed by the Hunter Service representative attached.

Charging for assembly or repair:
• The customer is charged the assembly fee when:
    - Specifically requesting to purchase a display model.
    - Requested to have a model assembled by the Hunter Service representative.

Activity Measurement
• All assembled bicycles sold have the Hunter Service Assembly Checklist removed by Customer Service Operator.
• Store copy of the checklist completed by the Customer Service Operator.
• Store portion of the checklist retained and forwarded to the Cash Office for filing.

Reference
Register Operations Manual, Sect. 2, page 44 – 45

Filing
Bicycle Assembly Process Registers to remain in the Bicycle Assembly Process Folder
