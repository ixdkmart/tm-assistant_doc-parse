Have you got Profit Protection Awareness?

Understand the Consequences

Internal theft & fraud of any kind, is a serious crime

Profit Protection have systems & monitoring measures to identify internal theft & fraud, with serious consequences that may include:

• Termination of employment.
• Legal consequences (police involvement, charges laid & you may have to go to court).
• Criminal convictions that could impact future employment, restrict overseas travel, completion of studies.
• Impacts to your fellow colleges, family & friends.
• Made to pay back what was stolen (restitution).
• You could be banned from entering a Kmart store.

SAY Something

Report suspicious behaviour:
A majority of our team live by our values and do the right thing. If you see something suspicious or unusual, please report this to your manager or through our Speak Up service. All matters raised are kept confidential, protecting those who raise concerns or allegations.

Ways to speak up:
• Talk to your line manager or store manager.
• Online: https://kmart.com.au/speakup.
• Phone (AUS): 1800 518 230 or (NZ): 0800 753 231.

Kmart Cares: Kmart has partnered with Converge (EAP) to support Kmart employees with personal challenges. This includes money management coaching and advice, to help you work through financial wellbeing concerns. We encourage you to speak with your manager or contact Converge/EAP on 1300 687 327
