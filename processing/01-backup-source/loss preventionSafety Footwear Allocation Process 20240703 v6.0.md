Safety Footwear Allocation Process

Guiding Principles:
This process applies to all Kmart Team Members completing tasks outlined in the PPE matrix that require safety footwear (refer to below for links to PPE documents on Kit). Team Members are only entitled to 1 pair of safety footwear per year through the below allocation options or reimbursements.

Footwear Allocation Options
Safety Footwear can be allocated to Kmart team members though the below options. Each option also requires the completion of the Safety Footwear Allocation Form via Kit (Stores > People > Teamwear & Team App).

Order Option | Order Method | Additional Resources

Kmart Stock
• Footwear must meet the standard listed within the PPE matrix.
• Allocated from store stock up to the value of $80 (sell price of stock).
• Recorded on the Safety Footwear Allocation Form (All fields filled out including adjustment information on form).
• Adjustments are to be processed on the PDT under adjustment Store Staff Uniforms. The quantity should be entered into the PDT as a positive and will allocate the footwear to code 31.

Petty Cash Reimbursement (AUS Stores)
• Reimburse the team member through petty cash (SCTF) up to $80 for Australian stores only and should only be actioned when the above footwear options are not suitable for the team member.
• Recorded on the safety footwear allocation form (Adjustment fields not required on form).
• Reimbursement is issued through Petty cash process (see cash management guide for process details).
• Recorded on the SCTF and the footwear allocation form. (a copy of the receipt should be attached)
• If the total of the purchase is over $80 the team member can only be reimbursed up to $80, so when completing the GTS calculation on line 27A this will need to be adjusted in line with the reimbursement.
• Footwear must meet the standard listed within the PPE matrix.

Petty Cash Reimbursement (NZ Stores)
• Reimburse up to $120 for New Zealand stores (this only applies every 2 years for NZ stores) other option will need to be used if already redeemed.
• Recorded on the safety footwear allocation form (Adjustment fields not required).
• Send the reimbursement form and copy of the receipt the Kmart Store Payroll.
• Footwear must meet the standard listed within the PPE matrix.

Safety Footwear Allocation Form Process

Step | Action

Step 1
Save a copy of the Safety Footwear Allocation Form (located on Kit under Stores > People > Teamwear & Team App).

[Form template shown with columns for: Store Number, Store Name, Date, Team Member Name, Employee ID, Badge Number, Footwear Keycode, Size (incl GST), Footwear Description, Stock Adj Date, Adjustment Team Member, Authorising Manager]

Step 2
Complete store details at the top of the Safety Footwear Allocation Form.

Step 3
If Footwear is "Kmart stock" ensure all fields are filled out including keycode and adjustment information. Ensure it is adjusted under 'Stores Staff Uniforms' on the PDT code 31. This should be done the day the footwear is issued.

Step 4
If Footwear is ordered from a supplier or a reimbursement given to Team Member, the keycode and adjustment information is not required as footwear that is not adjusted as store staff uniforms.

Step 5
If a team member is taking home the footwear and not wearing the footwear the day, it is issued a check seal should be attached to the footwear/ or bag.

Step 6
Set up a Tab in the Back Office filing system labelled 'Safety Footwear Allocation'.

Step 7
Place all items in this Tab in the back office filing system.

Step 8
Forms should be retained to ensure the store tracks what team members have been allocated footwear within the year.

Safety Footwear Returns/Exchange (Kmart Stock Only)

The Safety Footwear Returns & Exchange Form can be used when the Team Member needs to return the footwear if not fit for purpose or is deemed faulty, this form in only needed if the footwear was adjusted through Stores Staff Uniforms (code 31) "Kmart stock".

To add stock back to SOH on the PDT under Stores Staff Uniforms (code 31) make the adjustment +1, this will add the stock back to the stores Stock on hand.

EXCHANGED ITEM/S (- Adj)

Step | Action
Step 1 | Team Member returns safety footwear item to the Manager.
Step 2 | The Manager holds the item whilst the team member selects the correct item.
Step 3 | The Manager takes a blank 'Safety Footwear Returns Form' from the relevant Back Office filing system tab labelled 'Safety Footwear Allocation'.
Step 4 | The Manager fills in the Safety Footwear Returns Form and places it back in the relevant Back Office filing system tab to be adjusted the following Friday.
Step 5 | The returned item is placed back on the selling floor if fit for resale (change of mind) or if faulty placed into the RLO area for RLO assessment.
Step 6 | Set up a Tab in the Back Office filing system labelled 'Safety Footwear Allocation'.
Step 7 | Place all items in this Tab in the back office filing system.

What If Scenarios

Scenario | Resolution

A Team Member requires additional footwear but have already been issued 1 pair within the current financial year. | The Team Member can use the Team Wear purchasing option to purchase additional footwear.
Further details are located in the Kmart Team member Clothing Policies:

Policy - Team Member Clothing Discount (AUS)
Policy - Team Member Clothing Discount (NZ)

How can I check is Safety Footwear allocations have been processed correctly? | You can check the shrinkage report to see if Safety footwear allocations have been correctly processes on the PDT using adjustment code 31.
Search for code 31 on the shrinkage report by viewing workstation > Stock Management > reports > Shrinkage (by date range and adjustment code 31).

When do Team Members need to wear their safety footwear, and what happens if they forget to wear them? | Team Members must wear their safety footwear when completing any tasks that require them to wear the safety footwear as per the PPE matrix.
Team Member are not entitled to be issued with a new pair if they forget to bring in the pair allocated to them.

What happens if a team members safety footwear is worn out or deemed unsafe? | Unsafe or worn-out footwear must be replaced as per the PPE matrix.

Where can team members find information on uniform standards (including footwear)? | Team Members should refer to the Stores Teamwear Policy for further information located on Kit: Stores > Policies > Human Resources.
Policy - Stores Teamwear

Is the code 31 Stores Staff Uniform adjustment code used for anything else? | No, the code 31 adjustment code is only used for footwear outlined in this guide and the stores teamwear policy.
