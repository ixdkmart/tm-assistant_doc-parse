Printing RFID tags

1. Log into the RFID app

2. Select the 'Print' option from the main screen

3. To find the right item to print a tag for
• Scan the barcode or
• Enter using:
   * Keycode
   * Description
   * Style ID
   * If you enter the relevant detail to search, click on the magnifying glass to bring up the item

Apparel Tagging

Applying the RFID tag

1. Your RFID tags will come out of the printer as shown above

2. Once the RFID tags ready to apply to the front of the price tag:
• Ensure the RFID tag is placed evenly on the front of the tag without covering the tear line of the tag
• Tags can be placed horizontally or vertically
• It is recommended to check every 5-6 RFID tags after placing them on to confirm the RFID tags match the kimble tags

Tagging without a price tag

Blank rectangular cardboard tags and a tagging gun are supplied with your tools.
To connect a new tag to an item:
1. Get a blank tag and the tagging gun
2. Put on gloves
3. Remove safety cap from tagging gun
4. Place the needle through the hole in the blank tag and size tag, making sure both tags are pushed all the way to the base of the needle
5. Pull the trigger
6. Once the tag is secured on the size tag, apply the RFID tag

Bed Linen Tagging

Applying the RFID tag

1. Your RFID tags will come out of the printer as shown above

2. Once the RFID tags ready to apply to the front of the product:
• Ensure the RFID tag is placed evenly on the front of the product without covering the product information
• Tags should be placed horizontally.

Tagging without a price tag (Cushions)

1. Once the RFID tags ready to apply to the front of the care label:
• Ensure the RFID tag is placed over the barcode and not covering warning labels or product information
• Tags can be placed horizontally or vertically
