Digital Wallet Refunds Guide

Apple Digital Wallet Refunds (Mobile Phone Payments)
Step | Action

Step 1
The customer will need to open the Apple wallet application on their mobile device the payment was made on, and the select the card that was used for the payment (this can be done once the Apple wallet is opened).

Step 2
The customer needs to select the 3 dots on top of the displayed digital card to open more details about the device numbers linked to the card.

Step 3
The device account number listed will match the last 4 digits listed on the receipt. The card number listed will match the original card number listed on the digital card and the physical card, but this is different to the Device Account Number used for payment that is listed on the customer's receipt.

The last 4 digits listed as the Device Account Number will match the last 4 digits on the customers receipt if this is the same device used for the payment.

Google Pay Digital Wallet Refunds (Mobile Phone Payments)
Step | Action

Step 1
Repeat the above steps for google pay payments, however once the card the customer used for payment is selected the customer scrolls down to display the virtual account number.

The virtual account number will match the card number listed on the customer's receipt.

Digital Wallet Refunds Guide

Apple Digital Wallet Refunds (Apple Watch Payments)
Step | Action

Step 1
Open the Apple Wallet app and select the bank card used for the original transaction.
The Afterpay card can be found in the Apple wallet also.

Step 2
Scroll down and click on the card information, verifying the last 4 digits printed on the receipt match the device account number of the Apple watch.

Info
The device account number for the mobile phone and watch will be different.

Bankwest Halo Digital Wallet Refunds (Ring Payments)
Step | Action

Step 1
Inform the customer that the original payment method was paid using their 'Bankwest Halo' and it is required for the refund.

Step 2
Inform the customer they need to open and present their Bankwest app to match the last 4 digits of the payment shown on the receipt match up with the last 4 digits in the Bankwest app.

Samsung Pay Digital Wallet Refunds (Mobile Phone Payments)
Step | Action

Step 1
Inform the customer to open the Samsung Pay App, then select the 'Pay' icon so all the saved cards display.

Step 2
The customer can then select the card used in the original transaction and present to the Kmart Team member to match against the receipt.
The digital card number appears at the top of the digital card.
