SocialQ - Customer Check In

Hygiene
- Ensure you follow good hygiene principles and regularly clean equipment.
• Wash hands as required at regular intervals throughout your shift
• Wipe down equipment and bench with paper towel and anti-bacterial spray after every customer
• Wear disposable gloves and change them frequently across your shift, disposing of used gloves in a labelled bin with no lid
Equipment to be wiped down regularly.

Social Q
To use Social Q click and check customers in, you will need the following:
1 PDT or Tablet or Store Mobile Phone (https://client.socialq.net/login)

Process
Log onto SocialQ using Username, Password and store specific Pin Number

Select the 'Check In' button on the 'Today' page of SocialQ

Check In customer that does not have a smart phone to complete themselves.
• Enter customers first name and surname
• Enter Mobile Phone number (Land Line numbers are not accepted. A 10-digit number starting in '04' must be entered for the process to be completed)

OPTIONAL
• Enter customers email address
• Enter any additional dependents accompanying the customer
• Press 'Check In'

Note: If a customer is only able to provide an email address as they don't own a mobile phone, please enter '0400000000' to continue
