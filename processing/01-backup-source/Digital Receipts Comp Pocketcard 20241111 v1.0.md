## Page 1

Purpose
We're enabling our in-store team members to promote Digital Receipts and enhance Kmart app adoption, facilitating seamless returns and exchanges.

The incentive program also aims to raise awareness and educate customers about additional Kmart app features, including the price scanner, wish list, and the convenience of shopping anytime and anywhere.

For AUS Stores Only.

Promotion
In-store POS:
• Price check scanners
• Customer Service Desk signage
• Tea room poster
• Support card handout
• SCO bed decal

Promotion measured by: Digital receipt sends as a % of transactions

Customer Engagement
Team members should actively engage with customers at critical service touchpoints to highlight the advantages of the Kmart app.

• SCO Leader: Emphasize in-store promotion of the Kmart app and advocate for digital receipts by distributing support cards with instructions on accessing them.
• Assisted Checkout & FlexiPOS: When requesting Flybuys, inform customers that downloading the Kmart app will allow them to store digital receipts within the app.
• Service Desk: Encourage the use of the app while assisting with returns or exchanges.
• Shop Floor: If a customer requests a price or product info, promote the product scanner feature available on Kmart app.

Dialogue
• Digital Receipts: "Remember to scan your Flybuys and download the Kmart app to easily access your digital receipts whenever you need them through your account."
• SCO Sticker: "Don't forget to download the Kmart app and scan your Flybuys each time you shop, so you can always locate your digital receipts in your account."
• In-store Support Card: "Here's how you can download the Kmart app and access your digital receipt information."
• In-store POS: "Simply scan the QR code and follow the instructions to download the Kmart app."

Key Milestones & Dates
Team members should interact with customers at key touchpoints and highlight the advantages of the Kmart app.

• Launch Date: November 13, 2024
• Duration: Two weeks, ending on November 27, 2024 (concluding before the Black Friday weekend)
• Check-Ins: The top five stores in each zone will be reported every 3 to 4 days via Workvivo, showcasing the top-performing stores based on receipt percentages of sales. This will further enhance program enthusiasm and foster a competitive atmosphere.
• Conclusion: Winners from each zone will be announced during the week of November 29, 2024, through Workvivo.
• Prize: A $340 voucher for each winning store, or a line of credit added to the store's expense account.

Accessing Digital Receipts
1. Download and sign in to the Kmart app
2. Link your Flybuys card to your account
3. Scan Flybuys at checkout for easy digital receipt storage
