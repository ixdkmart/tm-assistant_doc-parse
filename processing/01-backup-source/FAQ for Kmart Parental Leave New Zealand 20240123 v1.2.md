Team Members FAQ - Preparing for Parental Leave

Under what circumstances can I take Kmart paid parental leave?

Eligible team members can take Kmart paid parental leave under the following circumstances:
• Giving birth to a child
• Primary or non-primary carer to the child (as applicable)
• Adoption of a child under the age of 6
• You have the primary permanent responsibility for the care, development and upbringing of a child under the age of six
• Stillbirth
• Premature birth
• In the tragic circumstances where a child passes away in the first 24 months of life

In the difficult circumstance where a team member or the team member's spouse / defacto partner experiences a still birth or miscarriage, they will be entitled to 5 days of bereavement leave in total, per occasion.

How do I apply for parental leave?

Let your Line Manager know you will be applying for parental leave at least 3 months before your anticipated start date. You will need to complete a Kmart Parental Leave Application form and submit it to your Line Manager along with either:

a)A medical certificate confirming the expected date of birth, or relevant medical circumstance; or

b) Documentation regarding the expected date of adoption of the child, or confirmation of primary responsibility for the care, development and upbringing of a child under the age of six.

I would like to work right up to the birth of my child, is this okay?

Yes, you may work up until the birth of your child, unless health and safety requirements mean working as normal would be unsafe for you or others .

Note: the responses above are short responses to some frequently asked questions. For more information, see the parental leave policy. In the event of any conflict between these FAQs and the policy, the policy will prevail.
