Adjusting Contrast on APEX Monitor

1) Press the first button on the bottom right of the monitor to display menu.

2) Press the 4th button to enter the Contrast/brightness Menu.

3) Press the 4th button to select contrast

4) Press the down arrow (2nd Button) to adjust contrast to 70%

5) Press the 1st button to save
