Circle In

A safe and supportive space for Kmart's caregivers no matter what stage of the journey you're on.

Log-in now by scanning the QR code

Circle In is platform for Kmart team members to support them on their caregiving journey. Whether you are planning a pregnancy, having a child or caring for a loved one you will find tailored support for you:

• Pregnancy, adoption and IVF assistance
• Childcare referral services
• Kmart's Parental Leave Resources
• Menopause support
• Wellbeing support
• Return to work resources
• Caregiving supports for new and expecting parents, working parents, parents of children with disability, caregiving support to other loved ones
• Inclusive supports reflecting families of all backgrounds and responsibilities
