Afterpay Card FAQs

What is Afterpay?
Afterpay allows your customers to take their purchase today, pay in four payments, every two weeks. Always interest-free.

What is the Afterpay Card?
Afterpay Card is a contactless digital card stored in a customer's digital wallet on their phone. The customer adds their Afterpay Card to their digital wallet via a one-time setup prompted in their Afterpay App.

How does a customer use their Afterpay Card to purchase in-store?
The customer can either initiate the transaction via the Afterpay app or launch the card directly from their digital wallet. The customer taps their Afterpay Card on your card terminal to pay and finalise the transaction.

How do I process an Afterpay Card sale in-store?
1. You process the Afterpay Card purchases the exact same way as you would process any other contactless card transaction (Eftpos, Visa or MasterCard).
2. The customer can initiate the purchase via their Afterpay app or directly via their digital wallet. To finalise the transaction, they simply tap their Afterpay Card on your card terminal to pay.
3. Transaction is complete.

Is there a minimum spend with Afterpay Card?
Some stores may choose to add a minimum spend with Afterpay. Great news, at Kmart there is no minimum spend with Afterpay in-store.

How do I refund to Afterpay Card?
You process Afterpay Card refunds the same way as you would normally refund to any Visa or Mastercard in-store.

The customer completes the refund by accessing their Afterpay Card directly from their digital wallet or via the below steps within their Afterpay app:
1. The customer opens their Afterpay App and selects the Card tab.
2. They click on 'How it works' above their pre-approved available to spend limit
3. They scroll down to 'How do I return an order?' and tap the 'Refund to Afterpay Card' button.
4. The customer taps their Afterpay Card on your card terminal to complete the transaction.

Note: If the original sale was made using Afterpay, the refund must go back onto the customer's Afterpay Card and no other tender type.

How do I ensure the refund goes back onto the customer's Afterpay Card?
Best practice is to cross-check the last 4-digits on the customer's Afterpay Card with the digits on the receipt.

Apple Devices
1. Open the Settings app.
2. Scroll down to Wallet & Apple Pay.
3. Tap the Afterpay Card.

Android Devices
1. Open the Google Wallet app.
2. Tap the Afterpay Card.
3. Numbers are displayed on the card or as a virtual account number.

## Page 2

Afterpay Card FAQs

Can I do a split tender sale with Afterpay Card?
Yes, If your business/store allows split tender transactions.
Enter the amount the customer wishes to spend on their Afterpay Card as a normal card payment transaction. The customer then taps their Afterpay Card on your card terminal. You then process the remaining balance on the other form of tender e.g. cash or other card.

What is the maximum amount a customer can purchase using their Afterpay Card?
This will be different for each customer. Customers typically have between $600-$3000 available to spend. The customer can view their available to spend amount on the card tab in their Afterpay app.

What happens if a customer is prompted to enter a PIN?
Easy! Sometimes customers will need a PIN after they tap Afterpay Card on the card reader, when this happens the customer can create or update their PIN easily within the Afterpay app. They simply click 'How it works' from the Card tab and set or update their PIN from the options.

What happens when an Afterpay Card declines?
If a decline error occurs, confirm with the customer that their pre-approved in-store spending limit is high enough to cover the purchase amount. This amount is visible via the Card tab in the customers Afterpay App. If issues occur, please contact the Afterpay Customer Support team.

How long will it take the customer to receive their refund?
Once a refund has been processed by your store, we instantly update the payment schedule and it may take up to 10 days for Afterpay to restore funds to the customer's original payment method. In some cases after your store has processed the refund, the customer will be prompted to follow a guided pop-up in our app to apply the refund to the original order.

What if the customers' phone doesn't support a digital wallet or NFC?
The customer can contact our dedicated customer support on 1300 100 729 and select option 6 to speak with an Afterpay customer service team member.

What else should I know about the Afterpay Card?
• Ensure you review your stores' internal policy on in-store Afterpay purchases.
• In some stores, there are items that Afterpay cannot be used to purchase with (E.G: gift cards or lay-by payments.
• We always recommend customers access their Afterpay Card from within their Afterpay App in order to know their pre-approved in-store spend balance and to access any in-app specific deals.
• The Afterpay Card is housed by MasterCard in Australia and by Visa in New Zealand, hence you process Afterpay Card sales/refund the same way as you normally process card transactions in-store.
