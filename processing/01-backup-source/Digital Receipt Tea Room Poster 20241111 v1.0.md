## Page 1

Digital Receipts via the Kmart App & In-Store Competition Details (AUS Stores)

Key Dates
Launch Date: Wednesday 13 November 2024
Duration: 2 weeks (ending prior to Black Friday weekend Wednesday 27 November 2024)

Connect your Flybuys account to the Kmart app and enjoy the convenience of digital receipts. Easily track your purchases and keep your receipts organised, making returns or exchanges hassle-free.

In Store competition details
The competition looks to reward the top store in each zone for issuing the highest number of digital receipt sends as a percentage of transactions, meaning any type of trading store can be a contender.

› Customers need to link your Flybuys card to the Kmart app and scan it at checkout.

› Store team members can share info cards to assist customers and ensure a smooth checkout. See POS material below.

A prize of $340 will be awarded to the winning store in each zone for Christmas and social team events. The store competition will be assessed based on: Digital receipt sends* as a percentage of receipt transactions.

*A receipt send is triggered by a Flybuys scan or linked Onepass account
