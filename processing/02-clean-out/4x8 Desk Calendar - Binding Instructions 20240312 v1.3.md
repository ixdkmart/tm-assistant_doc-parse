INSTRUCTIONS FOR MAKING DELICIOUS WAFFLES AND PANCAKES

What you will need:
• Ingredients for waffles and pancakes:
   o Flour
   o Eggs
   o Milk
   o Baking powder
   o Sugar
   o Butter or oil
   o Optional toppings (syrup, fruits, whipped cream)
• Cooking equipment:
   o Waffle iron
   o Non-stick frying pan or griddle
   o Mixing bowls
   o Whisk or mixer
   o Spatula

1) Prepare the batter by mixing flour, baking powder, sugar, eggs, and milk in a bowl until smooth.

2) Preheat the waffle iron and lightly grease it with butter or oil.

3) Pour the batter onto the waffle iron, close the lid, and cook until golden brown and crisp.

• Be careful not to overfill the waffle iron to avoid batter spilling out.

4) For pancakes, heat a non-stick frying pan or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease it.

5) Pour batter onto the pan to form pancakes of desired size. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.

6) Serve waffles and pancakes warm with your favorite toppings such as syrup, fresh fruits, or whipped cream.

Enjoy your delicious homemade waffles and pancakes!
