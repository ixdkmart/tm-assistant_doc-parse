INSTRUCTIONS FOR MAKING 4x8" DESK CALENDAR

What you will need:
• Monthly 4x8" calendar prints (produced on the D4600 duplex printer)
• 4x8" Calendar kit which includes:
   o Cardboard black frame x 1
   o Acrylic cover x 1
   o Binding wire black

1) Fold black cardboard frame on centre score line.

2) Pull out pins 1,2 and 25,26. Using the 23 pins not pulled out in between on the Calendar Binding unit, centre the black cardboard frame and punch holes through both ends of cardboard frame.

• Be very firm with the handle to ensure best result.

3) Do the same for the acrylic cover and calendar month, punching 4 pages at a time for best results, ensuring the pages are not mixed out of place.

4) Place prints on top of folded black cardboard frame, followed by the acrylic cover on top.

5) Thread black binding wire through frame, prints & cover

1) Clamp calendar wire shut by inserting it at back of calendar press machine and pulling handle firmly to close the wire coil. Do this slowly to ensure best result.
