Managing Broken Easter Eggs

Broken & damaged easter eggs contribute to large volumes of shrink at Easter. Follow each scenario below when this occurs:

Broken or half eaten easter eggs.
• Write the barcode on the soiled & damaged sheet & throw the eggs into the rubbish bin.
• When adjusting write off on the PDT under Soiled & Damaged.

Empty easter egg packets.
• If eggs are missing and only wrappers or packets are found, place the empty packets into the empty packet bin.
• When adjusting write off on the PDT under empty packets.

Packets of eggs with some missing.
• If packets of eggs have eggs missing, DO NOT EAT THEM OR SHARE WITH OTHERS. They must be written off & throw the eggs into the rubbish bin.
• When adjusting write off on the PDT under Soiled & Damaged.

Eggs received in delivery damaged.
• If eggs are received in a delivery already damaged DO NOT EAT THEM OR SHARE WITH OTHERS. They must be written off & throw the eggs into the rubbish bin.
• When adjusting write off on the PDT under DC Soiled & Damaged.
• If delivery arrives with a shortage adjust on PDT under Inner Carton Adjustment.

NOTE: This communication is for internal Kmart use only & is not to be shared externally through any means, including social media.
