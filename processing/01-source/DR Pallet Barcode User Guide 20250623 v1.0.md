## Page 3

Pallet Barcode Guide – During Decant & Sequence

During Decant
• When creating a pallet in decant, attach the pallet barcode template using a bulldog clip
• Using a whiteboard marker, write the decant date and pallet number.

During Sequence
• Team member to fill the pallet
• Scan the barcode to complete
• Runner or Sequence Manager to confirm barcode has been scanned
• Once scan confirmed, Runner or Sequence manager to remove the template
• Store in the folder for next decant
