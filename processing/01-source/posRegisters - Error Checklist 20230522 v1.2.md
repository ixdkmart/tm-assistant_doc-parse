Cash counts off / on compared to ensure count is correct, i.e. Register bag in correct register:

• Check last weeks K162 slip for Balance figure, under the Office count section. This should be the same figure.

Are all Cheques accounted for?
• Verify by checking all cheques listed on SATR balance to Register Clearance Book entries (NCM stores) / Clearance tab (CM stores).
• Cheques could still be in the register or with EFT validation slips.
• If you locate any, note the register number and pass to Office team to include with next day's work, this will then balance the register the following day.

• Verify the actual SATR for each register is used not the Recap Read.

Any Cash Office dockets for Clearance / Front End errors?
• Ask Cash Office members if there are any petty cash dockets being held in the Office that could relate to the registers; i.e. If you have a register short of $150 and they have a clearance error of $150 over, this could relate to your register.

Front End Cash Out
• Has the Cash Office actioned the EFT Cash Out form and to the correct register?

All Code 10 bags accounted for?
• From Escape call up all 'Code 10's' for that register for that day and check to slips held in Cash Office.
• Has all cash been removed from Code 10 bag. Ask Cash Office members / SAM to check all blue bags.

Void Previous (Total 10 Non Sales) actioned correctly (All registers except Lay-by)?
• All dockets should have 'Operation Complete'.
• Check this on Escape, if Transaction Voided is noted, you will have to process either as a refund or sale to correct.
• Check to see if a team member has tried to Total 10 a completed EFT transaction (including refund registers), as these cannot be voided. They have to be refunded and re-rung again. Or if on a refund register, resold then refunded again.
• Have voided cash E-vouchers on the refund registers been processed correctly?

Check under register drawer, around register area for cash or cheques.
• If you locate any, notate register number and hand to Cash Office to be included in the following day's work.

Has a refund been performed on a non-refund register?
• Check if the refund has a compensating sale. (Dept 95 transaction reversal).
• If not, why was the refund performed?

Has there been a void from the previous week corrected this week?
• Check with SAM to see if she / he have corrected errors from previous weeks.

Layby Registers:
• If you have a register error on a Layby register review escape to ensure all new charges have been processed correctly.
