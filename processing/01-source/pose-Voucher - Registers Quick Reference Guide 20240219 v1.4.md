e-Voucher quick reference guide: registers

Exchange only e-Voucher for product
1. Press total
2. Press e-Voucher key.
   Do not enter any amounts
3. Scan the barcode on the voucher
   (or type in the number)
4. The system will make sure the
   voucher can be used.
5. The transaction will complete
6. Give the e-Voucher and receipt to
   the customer.
   If there is no value left on the
   voucher – deface it and place it in
   the drawer to be included in the
   next clearance.

• Change – if there is more than $5 change to be given this will automatically go back onto the e-Voucher to be used again in the future. Less than $5 will be given in cash.

Exchange/refund e-Voucher for product
1. Press total
2. Press e-Voucher key.
   Do not enter any amounts
3. Scan the barcode on the voucher
   (or type in the number)
4. The system will make sure the
   voucher can be used.
5. The transaction will complete
6. Give the e-Voucher and receipt to
   the customer.
   If there is no value left on the
   voucher – deface it and place it in
   the drawer to be included in the
   next clearance.

• Change – all change on this voucher is given in cash.

e-Voucher cashed out/balance enquiry or reprint

When the customer requests the e-Voucher be cashed out, a balance enquiry or needs to have their voucher reprinted:
1. Press total
2. Press e-Voucher
3. Orange pop up box displays the
   following options
   1 – balance enquiry
   2 – e-Voucher cashed out
   3 – reprint voucher

Balance enquiry
Press 1 enter. Scan the voucher, the balance displays on the screen and prints a receipt showing the balance of the e-Voucher.

e-Voucher cashed out
Press 2 enter. Scan the voucher, the drawer opens to issue the cash and a refund receipt prints. Deface the voucher and place in drawer to be included in next clearance.

Reprint (managers key required)
Press 3 enter. Scan the voucher. New e-Voucher prints.

Please refer to the back of this page for any messages that displays on the register screen that varies from the above processes

The following table applies to both the exchange/refund e-Vouchers and the exchange only e-Voucher (the vouchers have the type printed on them)

If... | Then...
The whole voucher isn't used and it is an exchange only voucher | If the value is greater than $5 then the amount is returned automatically to the voucher. If the amount is less than $5, it is given in cash.
The e-voucher barcode wont scan | Type in the e-voucher number and press enter.
The e-voucher wont scan and you can't read the number | Call IT service desk (1800 033 534) you will need to provide the following details, date, time, register number, transaction number, and original issued amount of the voucher. Once you are given a number you can reprint the e-Voucher for the customer. Retrieve the transaction and finalise as per current process.
The register returns the following message: | 
• e-Voucher has expired | At a manager's discretion, the supervisor key can be used to override the error message and accept the voucher.

Offline messages

• e-Voucher temporarily offline | e-Vouchers are currently offline, call issuing store to confirm e-Voucher value. Follow the instructions on the screen.
• Phone for authorisation | Store is offline, phone IT service desk with the e-Voucher details for authorisation.

The full offline process can be found on ClickHome > Department > Stores > Stores - Operations > Stores - Front End > e-Voucher
