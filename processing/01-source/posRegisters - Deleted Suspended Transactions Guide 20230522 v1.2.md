## Page 1

DELETED SUSPENDED TRANSACTIONS

What is it for?
This report lists any suspended transactions that have not been retrieved and voided/used by close of trade.

What does it look like?
[Sample report shown with following columns:]
Deleted Suspended Transactions 28/01/09 03:05.         Page : 6

Register    Operator    Trans    Date        Time
8           705        2029     27/01/09    11:20
8           705        2027     27/01/09    11:18

What should I look for?

A. This report prints automatically each night. All reports must be retained.

B. All suspended transactions on this report must be discussed with the relevant team member to determine why they had not been passed on to the Customer Service Manager/ Front End Controller.

C. A Manager must sign all reports and document all investigation.
