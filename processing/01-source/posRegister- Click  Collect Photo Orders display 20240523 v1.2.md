Special Functions option on the Controller is used to display 'Click and Collect' and 'Photo' orders on a register

To activate a register to display 'Click and Collect' and 'Photo Orders' perform the following;

Enter a 'Y' next to the register that will be used to display 'Click and Collect' and 'Photo' orders.
