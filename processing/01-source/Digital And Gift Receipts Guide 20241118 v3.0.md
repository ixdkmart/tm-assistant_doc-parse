Digital & Gift Receipts

Digital Receipts

Scenario | Action
---|---
Which customer can get a digital receipt? | • OnePass customers who have linked to their Kmart account.
 | • Flybuys customer who have linked to the Kmart account.
 | • NAB customer who have opted into 'Slyp Smart Receipts'
Are there any changes to the instore process? | There will be NO changes to the instore process, and customers will still get a paper receipt. There will be instances where customers would show digital receipts for a refund. At this stage, it is unlikely a customer would exit with a digital receipt.
Are there changes to the refund process? | When requesting a refund, the customer may present a printed copy of their receipt or show a digital copy of the receipt directly from their phone.
 | If a customer presents a digital receipt for a refund on their phone, the barcode on the receipt can be scanned using the POS scanners enabling the refund to be processed as normal.
 | Similar to physical receipt refund process when a paper receipt cannot be scanned, team members can go to the refund screen and enter the details from the digital receipt into the POS to pull up the transaction and then process refund (example below: Orange = Transaction number, blue = store number, green = register number).
What to do if the barcode on the digital receipt won't scan? | [Image showing receipt and POS screen with labeled fields]
Info | Card verification: In case of card payments, digital receipt will show the last 3 or 4 digits of card number. Standard register paper receipts will show the last 4 digits of the eft card number.

Gift Receipts

Scenario | Action
---|---
When would a customer present a gift receipt? | Customer may present a digital gift receipt; this is sent to someone from the Kmart application and shared via email or a messaging service, the gift receipt includes the transaction details, as well as a scannable barcode.
How do I confirm the price paid for the gift/s? | Stores will need to validate the price paid for items by confirming the price that loads on the screen when scanning the receipt.
Can a gift receipt be refunded? | No, gift receipts can only be used to return items to an exchange voucher.
