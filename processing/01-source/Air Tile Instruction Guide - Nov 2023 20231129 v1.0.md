1. Remove the Air Tile from the clear protective sleeve & place it white side up

2. Place the alignment tool against one corner of the Air Tile. The tool must be in close contact with the tile along two sides.

3. Peel back 2 to 3 inches of the white release paper from the corner of the frame opposite the tool.

Crease the layer so the flap lays flat.

4. Put on the photo gloves & brush any debris off the back of the customer's photo to ensure it is clean.

5. Place the photo in the corner of the frame where the white layer has been peeled back.

Be sure to align to the corner
