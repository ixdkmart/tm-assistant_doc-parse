Troubleshootin' Common Issues

Refer to these tips when troubles be brewin' with yer waffles an' pancakes:

Issue - Batter too thick or thin  
1. Add a splash o' milk if too thick, like addin' water to yer rum  
2. Add a bit more flour if too thin, like patchin' a sail

Issue - Waffles or pancakes stickin' to the appliance  
1. Make sure the cookin' surface be well greased, slick as a sea snake  
2. Use non-stick spray or brush with butter afore cookin’

Issue - Uneven cookin' or burnt edges  
1. Adjust the heat to medium or medium-low, like navigatin' calm waters  
2. Cook for less time an' watch closely, like a lookout on the crow’s nest

Issue - Pancakes not flippin' easily  
1. Wait till bubbles form an' edges be set afore flippin’  
2. Use a thin spatula to gently lift an' flip, like handlin' delicate treasure
