## Page 1

INSTRUCTIONS FOR MAKIN' DELICIOUS WAFFLES AND PANCAKES, ARR!

What ye be needin':
• Ingredients for waffles and pancakes, matey:
   o Flour, the fine white treasure
   o Eggs, fresh from the hen’s coop
   o Milk, the creamy bounty
   o Baking powder, the magic leavenin’
   o Sugar, sweet as a siren’s song
   o Butter or oil, to slick yer cookin’ gear
   o Optional toppings (syrup, fruits, whipped cream) to make yer feast fit for a captain
• Cookin’ equipment, ye trusty tools:
   o Waffle iron, the iron chest of golden grids
   o Non-stick fryin’ pan or griddle, the flat deck for yer batter
   o Mixin’ bowls, where the magic brews
   o Whisk or mixer, to whip up the storm
   o Spatula, yer trusty blade for turnin’ and servin’

1) Prepare the batter by mixin’ flour, bakin’ powder, sugar, eggs, and milk in a bowl until smooth as the calm sea.

2) Preheat the waffle iron and lightly grease it with butter or oil, lest yer batter stick like barnacles to the hull.

3) Pour the batter onto the waffle iron, close the lid, and cook until golden brown and crisp as a treasure map.

• Beware, don’t overfill the waffle iron or ye’ll have batter spillin’ over like a mutiny!

4) For pancakes, heat a non-stick fryin’ pan or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease it, ready for the batter to land.

5) Pour batter onto the pan to form pancakes of desired size. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown, like the sun settin’ on the horizon.

6) Serve waffles and pancakes warm with yer favorite toppings such as syrup, fresh fruits, or whipped cream, and feast like the pirate king ye be!

Enjoy yer delicious homemade waffles and pancakes, arrr!
