# SocialQ - Customer Check-In

## Hygiene
Ensure good hygiene principles are followed and equipment is regularly cleaned.
Wash hands at regular intervals throughout the shift.
Wipe down equipment and benches with paper towels and anti-bacterial spray after every customer.
Wear disposable gloves and change them frequently across the shift, disposing of used gloves in a labelled bin with no lid.
Equipment must be wiped down regularly.

## Social Q
To use Social Q, click and check customers in.
You need a PDT, tablet, or store mobile phone with access to https://client.socialq.net/login.

## Process
Log onto Social Q using username, password, and store-specific PIN number.
Select the 'Check In' button on the 'Today' page of SocialQ.
Check in customers who do not have a smartphone to complete the process themselves.
Enter the customer’s first name and surname.
Enter a mobile phone number (landline numbers are not accepted; a 10-digit number starting with '04' must be entered).
Optionally, enter the customer’s email address.
Optionally, enter any additional dependents accompanying the customer.
Press 'Check In'.
If a customer can only provide an email address because they don’t own a mobile phone, enter '0400000000' to continue.
