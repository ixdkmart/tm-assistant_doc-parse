## Barcode Printing

How many types of equipment need barcodes?
Each piece of equipment will have two barcodes printed, one for each side.
For example, entering a quantity of 1 will result in 2 barcodes being printed.

Cage
Input field for quantity, then select Print.

Roll
Input field for quantity, then select Print.

Pallet
Input field for quantity, then select Print.

2. Enter the amount of barcodes you require for the equipment type. The maximum is 15; this will print a set of 2, totaling 30.
3. Insert stockroom sticker labels into the printer.
4. Press Print.

## Attaching a Barcode

H-Cages
H-Cages will have two barcodes on either side. These barcodes are supplier barcodes.
To replace both barcodes, reprint using the barcode printing steps.

Z Rails
Z-Rails require two barcodes attached to all rails.
Barcodes should be attached vertically as shown in the picture, on both poles of the rail.
To ensure the stickers remain attached, use clear tape to hold them down.
Wrap the tape around the pole and use clear online tape.

Pallets
Refer to the pallet barcode guide.
