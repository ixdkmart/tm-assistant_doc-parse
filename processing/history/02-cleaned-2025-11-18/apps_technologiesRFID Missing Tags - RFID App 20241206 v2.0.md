## Missing RFID Tags

What departments need RFID tags attached?  
The following departments are RFID enabled:  
Apparel, including Women's, Fashion accessories, Men's, Kids, Activewear, Footwear, and Footwear accessories.  
General Merchandise, including Bed Linen (Sheeting and Pillowcases, Quilt Covers and Sets), Blankets and Throws, Comforters, and Cushions.  

Items with EPC labels do not need to be tagged.  

How do I print RFID stickers?  
Open the RFID app, select Print, scan the barcode or enter the keycode, choose the printer, input the quantity, and click the printer icon.  
Tear the sticker from the printer, check the keycode matches, and place the RFID sticker on the Kimble tag.  

What if an item is missing the Kimble tag?  
Open the RFID app, select Print, enter the keycode, style code, or description.  
Select the correct size.  
Select the printer, input the quantity, and select the printer icon.  
While wearing gloves, attach the Kimble tag to the product using the needle gun as instructed.  

Where do I put the RFID sticker?  
Place the RFID sticker according to the visual guide, which shows placement for Apparel and General Merchandise items with specific instructions for different tag scenarios.  

What if the printer is not working?  
Refer to the Learning Guide in the Digital Stores Dashboard on the LMS to troubleshoot.  
Report the concern to your manager.  
The manager should follow the escalation process with IT if required.
