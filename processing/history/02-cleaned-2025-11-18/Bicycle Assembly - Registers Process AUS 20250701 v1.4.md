# Bicycle Assembly Process Registers

## Store Area

### Front End and Internal Registers

When work is performed, the customer presents an assembled bicycle at the front end or internal register for purchase.

### Activity Steps

Selling a bicycle:  
The Customer Service Operator must remove the Hunter Service Assembly Checklist from all assembled bicycles sold.  
The Customer Service Operator must complete the following information on the store copy of the Hunter Service Assembly Checklist using a medium black pen: purchaser's name, purchaser's address, phone number, purchase date, and store number.  
The store portion of the checklist must be retained and placed in the register drawer and forwarded to the Cash Office for filing.  
The $16.50 assembly fee is processed through the register as non-merchandise code 2-repair sale. Unless advised otherwise, all customers presenting an assembled bicycle for purchase are to be charged the assembly fee.  
Customers are not charged for any bicycle repair covered by the warranty conditions.  
An assembled bicycle must have an attached assembly checklist completed by the Hunter Service representative before it can be sold.

### Charging for Assembly or Repair

The customer is charged the assembly fee when:  
They specifically request to purchase a display model.  
They request to have a model assembled by the Hunter Service representative.

### Activity Measurement

All assembled bicycles sold have the Hunter Service Assembly Checklist removed by the Customer Service Operator.  
The store copy of the checklist must be completed by the Customer Service Operator.  
The store portion of the checklist must be retained and forwarded to the Cash Office for filing.
