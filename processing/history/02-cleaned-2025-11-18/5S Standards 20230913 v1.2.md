# 5S Standards

## Sort
Get rid of unnecessary items.

## Shine
Clean up the workplace.

## Set in Order
A place for everything and everything in its place.

## Standardise
Set standards.

## Sustain
Maintain standards.

The 5S process involves assessing everything in a space, removing what is unnecessary, organising items logically, performing housekeeping tasks, and maintaining this cycle. Organise, clean, repeat.
