# e-Voucher Quick Reference Guide: Registers

## Exchange only e-Voucher for product
1. Press total.
2. Press e-Voucher key.
3. Do not enter any amounts.
4. Scan the barcode on the voucher or type in the number.
5. The system will verify if the voucher can be used.
6. The transaction will complete.
7. Give the e-Voucher and receipt to the customer.
8. If there is no value left on the voucher, deface it and place it in the drawer to be included in the next clearance.

Change – if there is more than $5 change to be given, this will automatically go back onto the e-Voucher to be used again. Less than $5 will be given in cash.

## Exchange/refund e-Voucher for product
1. Press total.
2. Press e-Voucher key.
3. Do not enter any amounts.
4. Scan the barcode on the voucher or type in the number.
5. The system will verify if the voucher can be used.
6. The transaction will complete.
7. Give the e-Voucher and receipt to the customer.
8. If there is no value left on the voucher, deface it and place it in the drawer to be included in the next clearance.

Change – all change on this voucher is given in cash.

## e-Voucher cashed out, balance enquiry, or reprint

When the customer requests the e-Voucher be cashed out, a balance enquiry, or needs their voucher reprinted:
1. Press total.
2. Press e-Voucher.
3. An orange pop-up box displays options:
   1 – balance enquiry
   2 – e-Voucher cashed out
   3 – reprint voucher

### Balance enquiry
Press 1, then enter. Scan the voucher. The balance displays on the screen and a receipt showing the balance is printed.

### e-Voucher cashed out
Press 2, then enter. Scan the voucher. The drawer opens to issue the cash and a refund receipt prints. Deface the voucher and place it in the drawer to be included in the next clearance.

### Reprint (manager’s key required)
Press 3, then enter. Scan the voucher. A new e-Voucher prints.

Refer to the back of this page for any messages on the register screen that differ from the above processes.

## Additional instructions

The following table applies to both exchange/refund e-Vouchers and exchange only e-Vouchers (the type is printed on the vouchers).

If... | Then...
--------|----------
The entire voucher isn't used and it is an exchange-only voucher | If the value is greater than $5, the amount is returned automatically to the voucher. If less than $5, it is given in cash.
The e-Voucher barcode won't scan | Type in the e-Voucher number and press enter.
The e-Voucher won't scan and the number can't be read | Call IT service desk (1800 033 534). Provide the date, time, register number, transaction number, and original issued amount. Once a number is provided, reprint the e-Voucher for the customer and finalize the transaction as per current process.
The register displays the message: | 
• e-Voucher has expired | At a manager's discretion, the supervisor key can override the error message and accept the voucher.

## Offline messages

• e-Voucher temporarily offline | e-Vouchers are offline. Call the issuing store to confirm the e-Voucher value. Follow the on-screen instructions.
• Phone for authorization | Store is offline. Call IT service desk with e-Voucher details for authorization.
