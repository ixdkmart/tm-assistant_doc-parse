# Emergency Response Summary

## EVACUATION
"Whoop, Whoop, Whoop
Code Orange"

Evacuate the building immediately through the nearest emergency exit.  
Go directly to the assembly area.  
Stay at the assembly area until given the 'all clear' or further instructions.  
Always follow the Warden's or emergency services' instructions.  

## FIRE/SMOKE
"Beep, Beep, Beep
Code Red"

Await further instructions; you may need to evacuate.  
If evacuation is signalled, leave immediately through the nearest exit.  
Stay calm and go directly to the assembly area.  
If there is smoke, drop to your hands and knees and crawl to the nearest exit.  
Always follow the Warden's or emergency services' instructions.  

## EARTHQUAKE
"Code ERT, Code ERT, Code ERT"

Await instructions from the Emergency Response Team.  
If inside, do not leave until instructed.  
Move away from windows, outside walls, mirrors, light fittings, and heavy objects.  
Take cover by protecting your face and head.  
If outside, move to an open space or shelter under doorways.  
Always follow the Warden's or emergency services' instructions.  

## LOCKDOWN
"Code Lockdown, Code Lockdown, Code Lockdown"

Await instructions from the Emergency Response Team.  
Move away from affected areas as instructed.  
Utilise lockbox to secure entry if practical.  
Go to the Safe Zone and remain quiet to avoid attracting attention.  
If evacuation is signalled, leave immediately through the nearest exit.  
Always follow the Warden's or emergency services' instructions.  

## ADVERSE WEATHER CONDITIONS
"Code ERT, Code ERT, Code ERT"

Await instructions from the Emergency Response Team.  
Follow the Warden's instructions, which may include moving items to a safe location or placing sandbags or flood planks.  
If evacuation is signalled, leave immediately through the nearest exit.  
Go directly to the assembly area.  
Always follow the Warden's or emergency services' instructions.  

## CHEMICAL, BIOLOGICAL & RADIOLOGICAL
The beep, beep, beep warning tone and "Code ERT, Code ERT, Code ERT."

Standby and await instructions from the Emergency Response Team.  
Do not leave until unsafe areas are identified.  
Wardens will help you move away from danger if safe.  
If the emission is internal, evacuate the building.  
If the emission is external, stay inside and close all doors and windows.  
Always follow the Warden's or emergency services' instructions.  

## ARMED ROBBERY

Remain calm. Only speak when spoken to. Comply with demands and do exactly what you are told, such as handing over money.  
Observe the person and keep your distance if not directly involved.  
Avoid interrupting, adopting an aggressive stance, making eye contact, apprehending or chasing the offender, or raising an alarm.  

After the offender has left:  
Activate the alarm system. Secure the premises. Call Police on 000/111.  
Ask customers to stay until Police arrive.  
Restrict access to affected areas. Ensure no evidence is disturbed.  
Document everything about the offender.  

## BOMB THREATS & SUSPICIOUS ITEMS

Obtain as much information as possible using a Bomb Threat checklist.  
Identify any background noises.  
Remain calm. Do not hang up the phone.  
Inform the Chief Warden.  

If you find a suspicious item:  
Do not touch or move it.  
Have someone guard it to prevent movement.  
Do not use mobile phones near it.  
Immediately alert the Chief Warden.  
Always follow the Warden's or emergency services' instructions.
