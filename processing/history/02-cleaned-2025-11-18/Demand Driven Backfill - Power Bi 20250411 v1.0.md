### Metric Definitions
Fixture completion refers to how many fixtures have been finished versus the expected fixtures for the week.  
Average picking time is the mean time taken to complete a fixture, based on a standard of 19 seconds per keycode.  
Keycodes scanned indicates the number of keycodes scanned versus those expected.  
SMS integrity measures the SMS accuracy for the week and the rolling four-week period.  
Forecasted prioritized fixtures are the planned versus actual fixtures to be completed daily.
