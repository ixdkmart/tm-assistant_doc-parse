# Customer Account Review

## What is it for?
It is important to ensure that all customer accounts in your store's point of sale system have matching authorising documentation on hand.

8. You have printed a full list of the accounts that are available in your point of sale system.

9. Highlight each account on the screen and press enter to review the credit limit available. This credit limit must match the amount documented on the account holder's credit application form.

10. Other important items to check are:

a. All accounts must have a credit application form on hand.

b. The Store Manager can approve all accounts with credit limits up to $5,000 by signing off the completed credit application form.

c. Any account over $5,000 must have Head Office approval documented. All requests for limits over $5,000 are to be sent to Kmart Accounts Receivable mailbox (ar@kmart.com.au).
