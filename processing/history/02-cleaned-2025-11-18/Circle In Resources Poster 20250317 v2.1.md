# Circle In

A safe and supportive space for Kmart's caregivers no matter what stage of the journey you're on.

Log in now by scanning the QR code.

Circle In is a platform for Kmart team members to support them on their caregiving journey. Whether you are planning a pregnancy, having a child, or caring for a loved one, you will find tailored support for you.

Support includes pregnancy, adoption, and IVF assistance. Childcare referral services are available. Kmart's Parental Leave Resources provide guidance. Menopause support is offered. Wellbeing support is available. Return to work resources are provided. Caregiving supports are offered for new and expecting parents, working parents, parents of children with disabilities, and caregiving for other loved ones. Inclusive supports reflect families of all backgrounds and responsibilities.
