# Deleted Suspended Transactions

## What is it for?
This report lists any suspended transactions that have not been retrieved and voided or used by the close of trade.

## What does it look like?
The report shows a sample with columns including the date and time of the report, register number, operator ID, transaction number, date, and time of the transaction.

## What should I look for?
This report is printed automatically each night. All reports must be retained.  
All suspended transactions on this report must be discussed with the relevant team member to determine why they had not been passed on to the Customer Service Manager or Front End Controller.  
A manager must sign all reports and document all investigations.
