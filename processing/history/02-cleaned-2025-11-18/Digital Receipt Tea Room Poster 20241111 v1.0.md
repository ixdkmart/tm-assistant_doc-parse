# Digital Receipts via the Kmart App and In-Store Competition Details (AUS Stores)

## Key Dates
The launch date is Wednesday 13 November 2024.  
The competition runs for two weeks, ending before Black Friday weekend on Wednesday 27 November 2024.

## Purpose
Connect your Flybuys account to the Kmart app to enjoy digital receipts.  
This allows you to track purchases and keep receipts organized.  
It facilitates easier returns and exchanges.

## In Store Competition Details
The competition rewards the top store in each zone for issuing the highest number of digital receipt sends as a percentage of transactions.  
Any trading store can be a contender.

Customers need to link their Flybuys card to the Kmart app and scan it at checkout.  
Store team members can share info cards to assist customers and ensure a smooth checkout.  
See POS material below.

A prize of $340 will be awarded to the winning store in each zone.  
Prizes are for Christmas and social team events.

The store competition will be assessed based on digital receipt sends as a percentage of receipt transactions.  
A receipt send is triggered by a Flybuys scan or linked Onepass account.

## POS Material
Stores will receive various point-of-sale materials by 13 November to support the promotion of digital receipts.

SCO stickers must be applied as shown and should not interfere with Everseen.  
Handout info cards will be provided.

The materials include examples of existing POS materials and new POS decals and handouts showing how to download and use the Kmart App for digital receipts.
