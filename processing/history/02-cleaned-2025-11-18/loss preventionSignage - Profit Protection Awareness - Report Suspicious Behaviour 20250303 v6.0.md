# Have you got Profit Protection Awareness?

# Report Suspicious Behaviour

It is everyone's responsibility to be alert and vigilant in identifying suspicious behaviour.

If you see something suspicious or unusual, say something.

## Look out for signs of suspicious behaviour:
Look out for signs such as products or parcels being left in unusual locations. Empty packets, including in back of house areas. Witnessing a theft occurring. Leaving the store with stock that has not been paid for. Leaving the store without bags being checked.

## Report suspicious behaviour:
Report suspicious behaviour to your manager or confidentially through the Speak Up service. All matters raised are kept confidential.

## Ways to speak up:
Talk to your line manager or store manager. Online: https://kmart.com.au/speakup. Phone (AUS): 1800 518 230 or (NZ): 0800 753 231.

# Kmart Cares

Kmart has partnered with Converge (EAP) to support employees with personal challenges. This includes money management coaching and advice to help with financial wellbeing concerns. You are encouraged to speak with your manager or contact Converge/EAP on 1300 687 327.
