# Register Bags Stock on Hand Checks

Register bags require a stocktake count to ensure stock on hand (SOH) is accurate. 

# Register Bag SOH Check Process

1. Use adjust item on the PDT to complete the stock on hand check.

2. Do not count loose bags on the registers. Only count the bundles outside of boxes.

3. Ensure any open boxes are counted by the bundles inside and not by the box quantity.

## Singlet Bags

A box contains 7 smaller bundles inside. Each bundle has a quantity of 50 bags.

## Pillow Bags

A box contains 3 smaller bundles inside. Each bundle has a quantity of 50 bags.
