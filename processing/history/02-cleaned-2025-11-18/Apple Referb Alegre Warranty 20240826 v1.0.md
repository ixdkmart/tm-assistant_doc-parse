## 12 Month Warranty

Alegre devices are supported by a 12-month warranty from your original purchase date against defects and guarantees that cannot be excluded under the Australian Consumer Law. You are entitled to a replacement or refund for a major failure. You are also entitled to have the device repaired or replaced by Alegre if the device fails to be of acceptable quality and the failure does not amount to a major failure. This excludes faults caused through mis-use or mis-treatment of the device.

The 12 Month Warranty and 30 Day Satisfaction Guarantee are void in the following instances:

- Software that has been tampered with.
- Unauthorised repairs (3rd party repairs).
- Customer has failed to take reasonable steps to avoid the quality becoming unacceptable.
- Customer has used the product abnormally.

## How to return your device

To make a claim under the 30 Day Satisfaction Guarantee or the 12 Month Warranty, submit your request via https://alegre.net.au/kmartwarranty.

You will receive an Australia Post shipping label and instructions on how to package and post your device back to Alegre.

Once Alegre has received and inspected your device, they will facilitate a repair, replacement, or refund.

## Return Checklist

Ensure you have removed your SIM card and performed a factory reset before returning your device.

Alegre cannot accept a returned device that is iCloud locked. If your device is iCloud locked, you will receive a notification asking you to remove the lock via www.iCloud.com. If you do not remove the iCloud lock within 5 business days, the device will be returned to you and your return claim rejected.

Include a copy of your Kmart Receipt or Proof of Purchase with the returned device. Failure to provide this will result in your claim being rejected.

Return the same device as supplied by Kmart. Alegre will cross-reference the IMEI/Serial Number of your device against all devices supplied to Kmart. Warranty claims and returns will not be accepted if the original device was not supplied by Alegre to Kmart.

Claims submitted with incorrect information or without a valid proof of purchase will be deemed incomplete, rejected, and the device returned to you.
