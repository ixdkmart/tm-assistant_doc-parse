# Page 2

Backfill Fill - Task list

Select a task card. The task card displays your allocated workload, including tasks and area. Store tasks are listed with the date and location. Menu and Filter options are available. The task list shows the number of tasks to do and the areas H1 and H2.

A detailed task list will be displayed, showing progress as 0/8, with current time, estimated finish time, standard Time, and today's average.

For each area, the scheduled start time is displayed. For example, H1 at 7:00am with 20 minutes allocated, and 0/2 tasks completed. H2 is scheduled at 10:00am with 20 minutes allocated, with 0/2 tasks completed.

Version: 3.5

# Page 3

Backfill Fill – Completing a cage, rail, pallet

After filling the selected equipment type and area, scan the equipment barcode with the PDT.

Select "Complete Backfill Fill cage."

The equipment type and area will show as complete, and a pop-up message will display "done." Repeat these steps for each equipment type.

Screen details include progress tracking (e.g., 0/8, 1/8), current time, and equipment listings such as H1 Cage at 7:00 am with 20 minutes, H2 Cage at 10:00 am with 20 minutes. Confirm the scan with a message like "Scanned to H1," showing equipment details, e.g., UN200533733333. A completion message will appear, e.g., "Nailed it! H1 cage is done."

Version: 3.5

# Page 4

Backfill Fill – Editing a cage, rail, pallet

After filling the equipment type and area, scan the equipment barcode with the PDT.

Select "Edit area."

Select the world category.

Select the specific area.

A pop-up will display confirming the area has been updated. Select "Complete Backfill Fill cage."

The equipment type and area will show as complete, and a pop-up will display "done."

Screen details include progress tracking (e.g., 2/8, 3/8), current time, equipment codes, world categories (Home, Kids, Clothing, Event), and area categories (H1: Kitchen, Dining, Appliances; H2: Laundry & storage, Bath, Bed; H3: Pet, Furniture, Home & Auto Care, Decor, Outdoor; H4: Entertainment, Home office).

Version: 3.5

# Page 5

Backfill Fill – No area assigned for cage, rail, or pallet

After filling the equipment type, scan the equipment barcode with the PDT.

A pop-up will display "no area selected." Select "Complete Backfill Fill cage."

Select the world category.

Select the area.

A pop-up will show "Scanned to an area." Select "Complete Backfill Fill cage."

The equipment type and area will show as complete, and a pop-up will display "done."

Examples of options:

H-Cage: UN200533733333334

World options include: Home, Kids, Clothing, Event.

Area options include:
H1: Kitchen, Dining, Appliances
H2: Laundry & storage, Bath, Bed
H3: Pet, Furniture, Home & Auto Care, Decor, Outdoor
H4: Entertainment, Home office

# Page 6

Backfill Fill - Completing the task

If the last selected equipment type is displayed as "This is your last cage," scan the barcode with the PDT.

A pop-up will show "Great job on your last cage." Select "Finalize Backfill Fill."

A subsequent pop-up will state "Backfill Fill is now complete."

Version: 3.5

# Page 7

Backfill Fill – Additional messaging

If your workload has been adjusted, a pop-up will display.

If loadfill equipment is scanned, a message indicating "This is a loadfill cage" will be shown, and if completed, it will be added as a backfill fill task.

If the equipment scanned is not assigned to you, a pop-up will appear stating "This cage is not assigned to you." You may still complete the task, and an additional task will be added to your count.

Screenshots show progress indicators such as 3/11, timestamps, and confirmation messages as above.

# Page 8

Backfill Fill (Allocated) screen information

Displays total equipment types and progress, for example, "Progress: 0/8" or "Progress: 4/8."

Includes a field to type in the equipment barcode.

Shows current equipment times, such as 1 min and 6 mins.

Displays estimated finish times.

Includes equipment and area time standards.

Equipment details, for example:
H1 Cage, estimated finish at 7:00am, time standard 20 min, today's average 18 min.
H2 Cage, estimated finish at 10:00am (left screen) or 9:00am (right screen), time standard 20 min (left) or 10 min (right), today's average 12 min.

Progress indicators vary per screen, such as 0/6 and 0/2 on the left, 3/6 and 1/2 on the right.

Additional features include a numeric keypad for barcode entry and a bottom navigation bar.
