# Special Functions Option on the Controller

The Special Functions option on the Controller is used to display 'Click and Collect' and 'Photo' orders on a register.

To activate a register to display 'Click and Collect' and 'Photo' orders, follow these steps:

1. From the Controller Main Menu, select '2 Kmart Extended Application'.

2. Select '9 Special Function Registers'.

3. Enter a 'Y' next to the register that will be used to display 'Click and Collect' and 'Photo' orders.

The register will need to be reloaded for the change to take effect.
