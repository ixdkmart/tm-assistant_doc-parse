# Merchandise Protection - What Good Looks Like

Merchandise protection are security tags applied to high-risk theft products in stores to assist in the reduction of theft. All products listed in the Merchandise Protection Standards Spreadsheet must not be placed onto the shop floor without merchandise protection.

# Spider Wraps - What Good Looks Like

## Size
Choose the correct sized Spider Wrap for the product.

## Placement
Make sure the Spider Wrap covers all sides of the box. It should be in the centre of both the front and back of the product. The wrap should be tight and secure, but not to damage the box.

# Spider Wraps - What Not So Good Looks Like

## Size
Do not leave the Spider Wrap on too loose.

## Placement
The Spider Wrap must cover all sides of the product.

# Spider Wraps - Storage

## When You Don't Wind After Each Use
If you do not wind Spider Wraps after use, they become a tangled mess.

## When You Do Wind After Each Use
Wind after each use to easily access and apply for the next use.
