# RFID Tagging Learning Guide

## Printing RFID tags

1. Log into the RFID app.

2. Select the 'Print' option from the main screen.

3. To find the correct item to print a tag for, scan the barcode or enter details using keycode, description, or style ID. If you enter the relevant detail to search, click on the magnifying glass to bring up the item.

4. If you have used a keycode, style ID, or description, ensure you select the correct item. Once you've scanned the barcode, the item will appear.

5. Once the correct item is displayed, confirm it's the correct size. Click on the 'Select printer' option to choose your printer. From the list, choose the right printer and click 'OK'.

6. Return to the item screen. Enter the quantity of tags to print using the "+" or "-" buttons, or type in the quantity. Press the blue printer icon to print the tags. A maximum of 10 tags can be printed at one time.

7. If using a portable printer, detach the RFID tag and attach it to the relevant kimble tag, as per standard process.

## Apparel Tagging

### Applying the RFID tag

1. RFID tags will come out of the printer as shown above.

2. When applying the RFID tag to the front of the price tag, ensure it is placed evenly without covering the tear line. Tags can be placed horizontally or vertically. Check every 5-6 RFID tags after placement to confirm they match the kimble tags.

### Tagging without a price tag

Blank rectangular cardboard tags and a tagging gun are supplied with your tools. To connect a new tag to an item, get a blank tag and the tagging gun. Put on gloves. Remove safety cap from the tagging gun. Place the needle through the hole in the blank tag and the size tag, ensuring both are pushed all the way to the base of the needle. Pull the trigger. Once the tag is secured on the size tag, apply the RFID tag.

## Bed Linen Tagging

### Applying the RFID tag

1. RFID tags will come out of the printer as shown above.

2. When applying the RFID tag to the front of the product, ensure it is placed evenly without covering the product information. Tags should be placed horizontally.

### Tagging without a price tag (Cushions)

When applying RFID tags to the front of the care label, ensure the RFID tag is placed over the barcode and not covering warning labels or other product information. Tags can be placed horizontally or vertically.
