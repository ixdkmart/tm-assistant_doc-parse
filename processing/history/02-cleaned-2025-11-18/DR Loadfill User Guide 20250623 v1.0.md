# Loadfill (Allocated) – Editing a cage, rail, or pallet

To edit, scan the equipment barcode with the PDT.

Select "Edit area."

Choose the location from options such as Home, Kids, Clothing, or Event.

Select the specific area, for example, C1, C2, C3, or C4, with departments like Womenswear, Menswear, Footwear, etc.

A pop-up will confirm the area update. Then select "Complete loadfill cage."

The equipment type and area will show as complete, with a confirmation message.

# Loadfill (Allocated) - No area assigned for cage, rail, or pallet

After filling the equipment, scan its barcode.

A pop-up will indicate that no area is selected.

Select "Complete loadfill cage."

Choose the appropriate area options such as Home, Kids, Clothing, or Event.

Select the specific area, for example, C1 (Womenswear, W/SSH), C2 (Menswear Basics), C3 (Footwear), or C4 (Beauty & Accessories).

A pop-up will confirm that the equipment has been scanned to an area.

Update the status; the equipment will show as complete, with a confirmation message.

# Loadfill (Allocated) – Completing the task

When the last selected equipment type is reached, a message will state "This is your last cage." Scan the barcode.

A message will confirm: "Great job on your last cage."

Then select "Finalise loadfill."

A final message will confirm: "Loadfill is now complete."

# Loadfill (Allocated) – Additional messaging

If your workload has been adjusted, a pop-up will display a message: "Your loadfill workload has been adjusted."

If equipment is not assigned to you, a pop-up will state: "This cage is not assigned to you," or "This roll is not assigned to you."

In such cases, you can still complete the equipment type by selecting the appropriate button.
