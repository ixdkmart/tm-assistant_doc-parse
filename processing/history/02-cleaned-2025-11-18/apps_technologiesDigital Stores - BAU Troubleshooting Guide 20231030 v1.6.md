## Page 3

### TORY
- Mis-localised run | Clean TORY lasers and check wheels for debris. Check TORY run path map to identify where the problem occurred. Complete pre-run checklist prior to the next run.

- TORY is hitting shroud at the start of run | Clean TORY lasers and check wheels for debris. Raise with "Store Support" if consecutive runs fail due to hitting shroud at start of run.

- TORY keeps failing to dock after running | Check charging area for debris and clean if required. Return to charger. If problem repeats, raise with "Store Support".

- Lost TORY keys | Replacement TORY keys can be ordered via RTB (Spare Parts).

### HAND SCANNER
- Unable to connect to hand scanner to PDT | Turn on hand scanner by holding the power button (top right, green light) until a beep. Hold the Bluetooth button (bottom left, flashing blue light) until flashing. Log in to the RFID app, click on the hand-scanner symbol, select Zebra, start scan, hold yellow trigger, click the correct device ID, click yes to connect, follow prompts. The device will beep once successfully connected.

- Completing a hand scan to capture stock that TORY is unable to | Pair hand scanner as above. Refer to training guide.

### POWER PACK (Large printer)
- Seeing a triangle with an "!" on the screen | Unplug the printer from the AC power, turn AC power on, then plug the printer back into AC.

### PRINTER (Portable)
- Printer not connecting to network | Reboot printer in new area of stockroom or shopfloor. Contact store support.

- RFID label ordering | Order via Bluestar. Contact "Store Support" at 1800 033 534.

## Page 4

### PRINTER (Large)
- Issue: Printing out misaligned tags | Perform a manual calibration. Click menu on the printer, click the printer icon, click "Manual Calibration", click start calibration, follow the 16-step visual guide. | https://intranet.core.kmtltd.net.au/public/download.jsp?id=448654

- Issue: I cannot find the IP address | Click on "Printer Info" in the top right corner, IP address is under "Active IP (WLAN)". | https://intranet.core.kmtltd.net.au/public/download.jsp?id=448654

- Issue: The tags are sliding out to the right | Ensure the black lever (with yellow bottom) is pushed in as far as it can go to hold the tags in place. | https://intranet.core.kmtltd.net.au/public/download.jsp?id=448654

- Issue: The printer is printing out VOID stickers | Perform an RFID calibration. Click menu, click the RFID icon, ensure tags are fed out, click RFID calibrate, wait 5-10 minutes, then click the tick to finish. | https://intranet.core.kmtltd.net.au/public/download.jsp?id=448654

- Issue: The small calibration has stopped happening when turning the printer on | Go to settings, change "Power Up Action" to calibrate, restart the printer. | https://intranet.core.kmtltd.net.au/public/download.jsp?id=448654

- Issue: The small calibration stops happening when closing the printer lid | Go to settings, change "Head Close Action" to calibrate. | https://intranet.core.kmtltd.net.au/public/download.jsp?id=448654

- Issue: Printer is printing out faint labels | Go to settings, then Print Quality, set Darkness to 25-30.

### PRINTER (Both)

- Issue: RFID app not connecting to printer | Restart Printer and PDT. Contact "Store Support".

### RFID APP
- Issue: Team member requires password reset or a new account | Contact "Store Support".

- Issue: No pictures in backfill/product lookup screen | Reboot PDT, try an alternative PDT, raise with "Store Support".

- Issue: Application keeps crashing | Click "Wait", reboot PDT, try an alternative PDT, contact "Store Support".

- Issue: Backfill data will not load (TORY had a successful run) | Check TORY Dashboard for run criteria, reboot PDT, try an alternative PDT, then synchronize remaining data. Contact "Store Support" at 1800 033 534.

## Page 5

### RFID APP
- Issue: Backfill data will not load (TORY was interrupted) | Check Tory run quality; it must be over 90%. If under 90%, no backfill will generate. If over 90%, reboot PDT, try alternative PDT, then synchronize. If still no backfill, contact ITSD.

- Issue: Backfill data will not load (No run issues identified) | Verify previous steps, then click inventory summary tile. Ensure inventory % is above 40%. If under 40%, no backfill will be generated. Save and finalize, wait 15 minutes. If still no backfill, contact "Store Support".

- Issue: Backfill data will not load (Errors in inventory summary tile) | Check all previous steps. If error symbol is orange, data is uploading; wait up to 1 hour and try again. If red, data has failed to upload; raise with "Store Support".

- Issue: RFID app not on PDT | Raise with "Store Support".

- Issue: PDT freezes when loading data | Reboot PDT, try another PDT, raise with "Store Support".

- Issue: Incorrect store assigned to PDT | Remove battery, scan store-specific QR code.

### Store Operations
- Store has not moved TORY to second location in time | Check if backfill has loaded. Set reminders. Contact store support if a later schedule is needed.

- Strip and seal / overnight works in store | Notify ZM and store support. Engage EM stop buttons.

- Store has not left BOH doors open for TORY | Ensure pre-run check is completed and doors are left open daily.

- TORY's pathway blocked by equipment or congestion | Ensure pre-run check is completed and pathway is clear.

- Stock left in wrong container (e.g., returns in SHL) and generates backfill | Locate and fill stock where possible. Coach team to leave stock in correct containers. Contact "Store Support" for container adjustments.

- TORY not on charge correctly | Ensure pre-run checks confirm charge status. Contact management if issues persist.

**Contact "Store Support" at 1800 033 534**
