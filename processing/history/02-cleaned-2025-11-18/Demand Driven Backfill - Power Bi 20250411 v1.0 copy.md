### Metric Definitions  
Fixture Completion indicates how many fixtures have been completed and the total fixtures expected to be completed for the week.  
Average Picking Time is the average time taken to complete a fixture, based on the business time standard of 19 seconds per keycode.  
Keycodes Scanned shows how many keycodes have been scanned versus how many were expected to be scanned.  
SMS Integrity reflects SMS integrity results for the previous week and the rolling four-week period.  
Forecasted Prioritized Fixtures are the forecasted and actual fixtures to be completed for the week, broken down by day.  
Backfill Results provide an overview of backfill outcomes for the current week.
