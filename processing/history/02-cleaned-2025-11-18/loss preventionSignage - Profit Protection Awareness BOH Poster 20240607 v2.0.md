# Profit Protection Awareness

It is important to pay attention to all areas of the store, including back of house areas.

If you suspect anything unusual, follow these steps:

See  
Look out for anything that appears unusual or out of place, such as empty packets or concealed stock.

Act  
Note the location of the items.

Report  
Notify a manager or call the Speak Up Hotline.
