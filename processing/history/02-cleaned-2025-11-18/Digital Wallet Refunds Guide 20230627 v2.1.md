# Digital Wallet Refunds Guide

## Apple Digital Wallet Refunds (Mobile Phone Payments)

Step 1  
The customer will need to open the Apple Wallet application on their mobile device the payment was made on.  
Then, select the card that was used for the payment.

Step 2  
The customer needs to select the three dots on top of the displayed digital card to open more details about the device numbers linked to the card.

Step 3  
The device account number listed will match the last four digits listed on the receipt.  
The card number listed will match the original card number listed on the digital card and the physical card.  
This is different from the Device Account Number used for payment, which is listed on the customer's receipt.  
The last four digits listed as the Device Account Number will match the last four digits on the receipt if this is the same device used for the payment.

## Google Pay Digital Wallet Refunds (Mobile Phone Payments)

Step 1  
Repeat the above steps for Google Pay payments.  
Once the card used for payment is selected, scroll down to display the virtual account number.

Step 2  
The virtual account number will match the card number listed on the customer's receipt.

## Apple Digital Wallet Refunds (Apple Watch Payments)

Step 1  
Open the Apple Wallet app and select the bank card used for the original transaction.  
The Afterpay card can be found in the Apple Wallet as well.

Step 2  
Scroll down and click on the card information.  
Verify that the last four digits printed on the receipt match the device account number of the Apple Watch.

The device account number for the mobile phone and watch will be different.

## Bankwest Halo Digital Wallet Refunds (Ring Payments)

Step 1  
Inform the customer that the original payment method was paid using their Bankwest Halo account.  
It is required for the refund.

Step 2  
Inform the customer they need to open and present their Bankwest app.  
The last four digits of the payment shown on the receipt must match the last four digits in the Bankwest app.

## Samsung Pay Digital Wallet Refunds (Mobile Phone Payments)

Step 1  
Inform the customer to open the Samsung Pay app.  
Select the 'Pay' icon so all saved cards display.

Step 2  
The customer can then select the card used in the original transaction.  
Present it to the Kmart team member to match against the receipt.  
The digital card number appears at the top of the digital card.

## Samsung Pay Digital Wallet Refunds (Watch Payments)

Step 1  
Open the Samsung app and choose the bank card used in the original purchase.  
The Afterpay card can also be found in this app.

Step 2  
Scroll down and click on the card information.

Step 3  
Verify the last four digits on the receipt match the digital card number displayed on the smartwatch.
