# How does Kmart Group support its team members and communities?

Answer:
Kmart Group values respect, fairness, diversity, and inclusion. We support charities like Salvation Army and RizeUp. We commit to sustainability by using renewable energy and reducing waste. We aim to create a safe, supportive workplace for all team members.

Tip: Get involved in local community events and sustainability initiatives to make a positive impact.

Why/When: Because respecting people and communities helps us work better together and support growth.
