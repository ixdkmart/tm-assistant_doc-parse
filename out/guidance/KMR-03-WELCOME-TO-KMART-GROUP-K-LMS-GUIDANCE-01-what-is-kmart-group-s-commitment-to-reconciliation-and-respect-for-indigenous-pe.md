# What is Kmart Group's commitment to reconciliation and respect for Indigenous peoples?

Answer:
Kmart Group respects Aboriginal, Torres Strait Islander, and Indigenous peoples worldwide. We commit to equitable opportunities and a welcoming environment. We use our reach to drive positive change and honour traditional custodians of culture and heritage.

Tip: Remember, respecting Indigenous cultures helps create a positive workplace for everyone.

Why/When: Showing respect supports reconciliation and builds stronger community connections.
