# What is Kmart Group's purpose and how does it impact team members and customers?

Answer:
Kmart Group's purpose is making everyday living brighter for customers and team members. We believe everyone deserves great products at affordable prices. Our brands—Kmart, Target, and Anko—bring this vision to life every day.

Tip: Remember, your role helps make life better for customers and your team.

Why/When: Knowing our purpose helps you connect with our goals and values.
